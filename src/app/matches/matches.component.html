THE 6IXTY, a new 60-ball tournament, was unveiled jointly by the Cricket West Indies and the Caribbean Premier League on Wednesday (June 22). The inaugural edition, set to be played between August 24 and 28 at Warner Park in St Kitts & Nevis, is expected to feature men's and women's teams compete to a new set of playing conditions.
